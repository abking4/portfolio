Background

I'm currently an independent contractor based in Birmingham, AL. I graduated from Mississippi College University with a degree in Graphic Design and a minor in Marketing. After graduation, I worked with Mad Genius Agency in Ridgeland, MS, supporting clients like Superior Catfish, Memorial Hospital, and Southern Farm Bureau. Today, I combine my design background with front-end development to create accessible, thoughtful experiences—driven by a personal passion for building tools that empower people of all abilities.

As a front-end developer with a background in UI/UX design, I thrive at the intersection of form and function. I’m passionate about crafting interfaces that are intuitive, accessible, and performant — aligning thoughtful design with clean, scalable code. I care deeply about making information and tools more accessible, empowering people to learn, grow, and improve their quality of life through better digital experiences.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, I'm probably snowboarding, cruising around on my penny board, or crossing off another item on my bucket list.
